Privacy & Confidentiality

Your trust is our priority—here’s how we protect your information.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 4, 2025

At Get IT Consulting, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (get-it-consulting.com), use our IT metrics assessment, schedule consultations via Calendly, or make payments through Stripe.

What We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and company details (if provided) when you schedule a consultation or contact us. We recommend using a personal email address to minimize visibility to your employer or colleagues.
  • IT Metrics Data: Information you provide in our metrics assessment tool (e.g., SLA, cost, procurement data), used solely to generate your performance report.
  • Payment Information: Processed securely via Stripe; we do not store your payment details. Receipts are sent separately and labeled discreetly (e.g., "[Calendly] Strategy Review Kick-Off Call with Get IT Consulting").
  • Usage Data: Analytics on how you interact with our site, collected via Cloudflare for performance improvements.

How We Use Your Information

  • To schedule and confirm consultations via Calendly.
  • To process payments securely through Stripe.
  • To generate IT metrics reports for your use.
  • To improve our services and website functionality.

How We Protect Your Information

  • We use secure, industry-standard encryption for data transmission.
  • We do not share your information with third parties, including your employer or colleagues, except as required for scheduling (Calendly) and payments (Stripe).
  • Analytics data is anonymized and used solely for improving our services.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at info@get-it-consulting.com.

Confidentiality Statement (NDA)

At Get IT Consulting, we understand the sensitive nature of the IT and business information you share during consultations. We commit to the following:

  • Non-Disclosure: All information shared during consultations, including IT metrics, business strategies, and operational details, will be kept strictly confidential and not disclosed to any third party—including your employer or colleagues—without your explicit consent, except as required by law.
  • Use of Information: Your information will only be used to provide the consulting services you’ve engaged us for (e.g., IT strategy reviews, ongoing guidance).
  • Secure Handling: We use secure methods to store and communicate your information, ensuring it remains protected.
  • Duration: This confidentiality commitment remains in effect during and after our engagement, indefinitely, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

For a formal Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA), please contact us at info@get-it-consulting.com to request a signed document tailored to your needs.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our privacy practices or confidentiality commitments, please reach out at info@get-it-consulting.com.
